Technical Support

Here are some common technical questions and their answers.

Questions

  • How do I reset my password?
  • Why is my account not working?
  • How do I update the app?

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions sent to your email.

Why is my account not working?

If your account is not working, ensure you are using the correct login credentials. If the issue persists, contact support.

How do I update the app?

You can update the app through your device's app store. Search for the app and click "Update."
