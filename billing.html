Billing & Payment

Here are some common billing and payment questions and their answers.

Questions

  • How do I update my payment information?
  • Can I get a refund?
  • Where can I download my invoice?

How do I update my payment information?

You can update your payment information in the "Billing" section of your account settings.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Where can I download my invoice?

You can download your invoice from the "Billing" section of your account.
